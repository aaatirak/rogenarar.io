EVE

the Iron Queen

Eve was once trained as a hunter for the Sparnian government until lately discovered she was only being used and known to be killed as soon as she finishes her duty. With that, she then refuged and left the capital. Joining the mercenary troops, she was a great use due to her capability of sending steel agony to her enemies.
