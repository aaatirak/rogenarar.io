INYA

the Nature's Attendant

Deeper inside the Sparnian forest lies the silent dryad who keeps on chanting harmonic songs. Inya attracts nearby living beings and adores her passion. And when danger comes, Inya's precision comes at first, hurling an enchanted spear that becomes harmful at a distant. Despite of being wounded in battle, Inya's cure is the best of all.
