MEA

the Sorceress of Light

When darkness stands against the light, Mea is the one whom to be called. With her radiant spells, she can vanquish away dark beings at an instant. Such purifying essence that Mea bestows greatly provokes healing touch to her allies. She relies on her casting skills for her to survive in the Mathoran plains and begins wandering in search for her new home.

**********************************************

CLASSIFICATION: Mage

Health: 465 (+40)
Mana: 220 (+45)
Attack Damage: 42 (+2)
Armor: 3 (+1 per 4 level)

Health Regen: 1.5 (+0.2)
Mana Regen: 1.3 (+0.2)
Attack Time: 1.40s (+2%)
Magic Resist: 15%

Attack Range: 600
Movespeed: 270

Essence Of Light
Slot: E
Type: Passive

Healing Percentage: 20% / 30% / 40% / 50%
