XET

the Plague Hunter

Mostly called shooter-for-hire maniac. Upon given a target, Xet slowly sneaks his next victim to suffer great poison, crawling away from him, and begging for mercy. Xet specialize at cauterizing a single victim and indeed drowning it with venom. Everytime people hear the name Xet, for sure they'll start making panic.
