GRUYU

the Boar Headhunter

Gruyu scouts the wall side of his village, guarding and protecting his tribe from the dire humans who keeps on attacking his on kind. If battle may created, Gruyu seeks the upper part of his foe and shoots razor-sharp spikes unto his enemy and leaving him ignored will cause too much pain on its wake.

**********************************************

CLASSIFICATION: Shooter

Health: 490 (+40)
Mana: 210 (+15)
Attack Damage: 47 (+3)
Armor: 3 (+1 per 3 level)

Health Regen: 2.0 (+0.2)
Mana Regen: 1.2 (+0.2)
Attack Time: 1.15s (+3%)
Magic Resist: 15%

Attack Range: 550
Movespeed: 280

ABILITIES



Unleash Spine

Headhunter's Blood
Slot: W
Type: Instant Cast
Temporarily gains bonus attack speed each time Gruyu uses an ability.
Attack Speed Bonus: 30% / 40% / 50% / 60%
Bonus Duration: 4 seconds

When activated, Gruyu regains health instantly at the cost of his mana.
Heal Amount: 50 / 75 / 100 / 125
---

Razor-sharp Missle
Slot: E
Type: Passive
Every third attack at the same target, Gruyu's sharp spine pierces through the target's armor, dealing bonus physical damage based on the target's maximum health.
Max. Health Percentage: 4.5% / 6% / 7.5% / 9%

Sends out spikes which erupts from the ground, shooting spiked tendrils out in a straight line with a massive distance, dealing magical damage and hurling enemies into the air in their wake, stunning them for a brief period.
Damage: 150 / 200 / 250

Stun Duration: 1.5 seconds
---
