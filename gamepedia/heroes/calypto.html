CALYPTO

the Void Walker

Calypto was an incarnate of the void palace, abandoned his place and travelled between space. With such negative energies, Calypto protects himself with null spheres which negates any incoming harm and counters it with weakening force. The void walker himself is hard to find and is semi-impossible to be caught.
