**********************************************


REYSTAR

the Rifleman

A back-to-back champion of the Mathoran sharpeye competition and an undefeated accuracy master, Reystar was known as a one-shot dead personel due to his great aiming skills. With his flexibility, he also acts like a teeming feather in a pinch. A bullet with his true being brings sweetheart to his hand.

**********************************************

CLASSIFICATION: Shooter

Health: 510 (+40)
Mana: 210 (+25)
Attack Damage: 50 (+2)
Armor: 3 (+1 per 4 level)

Health Regen: 1.7 (+0.2)
Mana Regen: 1.4 (+2)
Attack Time: 1.30s (+2%)
Magic Resist: 15%

Attack Range: 550
Movespeed: 270

**********************************************


ABILITIES



Phosphorous Rocket
Slot: Q
Type: Area Target
Launches a rocket at a target location, dealing magical damage to enemies hit and provides vision in that area for 5 seconds. Invisible enemies are also revealed in that area.
Damage: 60 / 120 / 180 / 240
Reveal AoE: 450
---
Cooldown: 9 / 8 / 7 / 6 seconds
Cost: 50 mana


Draw A Bead
Slot: W
Type: Instant Cast
Reystar quickly strafes backward, reduces incoming damage taken from enemies for 2 seconds and procs Innershell Bullet for the next attack.
Damage Reduction: 25% / 30% / 35% / 40%
---
Cooldown: 12 / 10 / 8 / 6 seconds
Cost: 35 / 40 / 45 / 50 mana


Innershell Bullet
Slot: E
Type: Passive
Every 7th attack, Reystar's rifle pumps out, causing his normal attack to deal bonus physical damage.
Damage Multiplier: 1.8 / 2.0 / 2.2 / 2.4


Take Aim
Slot: R
Type: Point Target
Begins charging Reystar's rifle then takes aim in his target direction. After channeling for a brief period, Reystar then unleashes a mega bullet which deals physical damage to the first enemy hero hit.
Damage: 600 / 750 / 900
---
Cooldown: 90 seconds
Cost: 125 mana
