**********************************************


NLVASH

the Forest Spirit

A lone spirit wandering the middle forest of Mathora. A little spark within him brightens the forest day and night. Being able to communicate with suche eerie voice chills the bones of nearby living creatures. As friendly as it seems, the little spirit cures an ailment and makes his patient alive than ever.

**********************************************

CLASSIFICATION: Support

Health: 490 (+40)
Mana: 290 (+35)
Attack Damage: 43 (+2)
Armor: 2 (+1 per 4 level)

Health Regen: 1.9 (+0.3)
Mana Regen: 1.2 (+0.3)
Attack Time: 1.10s (+2%)
Magic Resist: 15%

Attack Range: 500
Movespeed: 285

**********************************************


ABILITIES



Will of Burst
Slot: Q
Type: Unit Target
Nlvash sprungs the living embodiment of his target, dealing magical damage around the target enemy while knocking Nlvash back.
Damage: 75 / 125 / 175 / 225
Knockback Range: 400
---
Cooldown: 10 / 8 / 6 / 4 seconds
Cost: 50 mana


Spirit Touch
Slot: W
Type: Unit Target
Links unto a living creature, restoring an amount of health to both Nlvash and his target unit based on its missing health. Nlvash cannot target Spirit Touch to himself, instead he can target an ally or enemy in order to gain effect of Spirit Touch while the enemy won't gain heal effect.
Heal Amount: 40 / 60 / 80 / 100
Missing Health As Heal Bonus: 5% / 7% / 9% / 11%
---
Cooldown: 6 / 5 / 4 / 3 seconds
Cost: 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 mana


Nearby Spirit
Slot: E
Type: Passive
Nlvash's outer spheric body conjures an enchanted presence, amplifying the magic damage taken by nearby enemies while unleashing random spirit missle that attacks random enemies.
Magic Amplification: 10% / 15% / 20% / 25%
Damage Per Spirit: 15 / 30 / 45 / 60
Spirit Attack Interval: 2.75 / 2.25 / 1.75 / 1.25 seconds


Guardian Impact
Slot: R
Type: Point Target
The forest spirit inhales powerful energies around him and dashes towards the target location and stops upon hitting en enemy. Enemies around it will take magical damage and are rooted for a short period.
Damage: 150 / 225 / 300
Root Duration: 2 / 2.5 / 3 seconds
---
Cooldown: 120 seconds
Cost: 150 mana
