ENT

the Corrupted Treant

When there is good, there is evil. Half side of the Sparnian forest is corrupted and is where Ent lies. The wise treant keeps his den more autonomous, allowing trespassers to take not less than a moment to leave his place. Ent survives by sending his dear minions unto a nearby place to hunt some of his prey and in return of sustainability.
