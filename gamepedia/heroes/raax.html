Ra'ax

the Ghoul

A wild creature wandering around the outskirts of Outloss, Ra'ax sustains his undead body by feeding at the helpsless victims caught walking near him, lunging towards it and consumes the pitiful creature without leaving a trace. Ra'ax also thirsts for blood and senses nearby, providing him a location for his next prey, making him a hunter of blood.
