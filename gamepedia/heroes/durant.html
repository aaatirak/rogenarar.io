DURANT

the Crusader

"With great courage comes great reward and honor for thee, sparnia!", Durant's inspiring words warmed the heart of all warriors in the kingdom. As the King promoted him to a loyal crusader, he was then transformed into a powerful warrior. Facing enemies without fear and such strength cannot tackle Durant alone.
